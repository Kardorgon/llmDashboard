# Sales Playbook Notes

Regional revenue growth should be compared with deal volume and churn.
If a region has high revenue but also high churn, account managers should review contract fit and onboarding quality.
If deal count rises while revenue stays flat, discounting may be too aggressive.

Recommended actions:
- Focus expansion campaigns on regions with low churn and stable support load.
- Trigger churn-prevention outreach for segments above 4 percent monthly churn.
- Review support escalation trends before launching upsell campaigns.
